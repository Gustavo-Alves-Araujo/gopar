# Images Required

Place the following image files in this directory:

## Logos
- `logo.png` — Agrosn logo (dark, for white backgrounds)
- `logo-branco.png` — Agrosn white logo (for dark/transparent backgrounds)
- `agro-logo.png` — Agrosn secondary logo (used in About section watermark and Footer)

## Hero Section
- `hero.jpg` — Hero background image (desktop, full-width)
- `hero-mobile.jpg` — Hero background image (mobile)

## About Section
- `agro-img.png` — About section photo

## Services Section
- `services.jpg` — Services section photo

## Products Section
- `gorduras.png` — Gorduras (fats) product image
- `proteinas.png` — Proteínas (proteins) product image
- `oleos.png` — Óleos (oils) product image
- `farelos.png` — Farelos (brans) product image

## Diferentials Section
- `diff-logo.png` — Decorative logo (displayed with low opacity)
- `diff-foto.jpg` — Diferentials section photo

## Other
- `check.png` — Check/tick icon (used in Diferentials list items)
- `contact.jpg` — Contact section decorative image
